# YASCO ERP — New Features & Enhancements

## 1. **3D Animated Landing Page**
- Three.js powered particle field background with 2000+ animated particles
- 3D wireframe globe with smooth rotation via `@react-three/fiber`
- Orbiting rings with dynamic rotation effects
- Scroll-triggered entrance animations (ScrollReveal component)
- Enhanced 3D Module Cards with real-time mouse-tracking parallax and glare effects
- 3D perspective transforms on hover (rotateX/rotateY based on cursor position)

- Retail POS with barcode scanning, touchscreen support, and offline mode
- Restaurant POS with table management and order splitting
- Pharmacy POS with prescription tracking
- Wholesale POS with bulk pricing
- Shift management for multi-operator environments
- Hold/Resume transactions, split payments, returns/exchanges
- Thermal and A4 printer support
- QR-based tax invoice generation
- Loyalty points integration
