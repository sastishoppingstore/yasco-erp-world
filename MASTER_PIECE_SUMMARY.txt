🤖 AI CAPABILITIES - COMPLETE INTELLIGENCE

1. INSIGHTS GENERATION
   Input: Budget %, Schedule %, Quality Score, Safety Score
   Output: 5 specific actionable insights + risks + recommendations
   Example: "Budget spending is 4% over schedule - accelerate cost control"

2. BUDGET FORECASTING
   Input: Historical spending data, daily average
   Output: Final project cost forecast, completion date, risk level
   Example: "Project will exceed budget by 12% if spending continues"

3. RISK ANALYSIS
   Input: Risk factors (weather, materials, labor, inspections)
   Output: Risk levels (1-10) + impacts + mitigation strategies
   Example: "Material shortage risk: 8/10 - recommend 2-week buffer stock"

4. DOCUMENT ANALYSIS
   Input: Bid, invoice, contract, report, or RFI
   Output: Summary, key items, issues, compliance, actions, cost/timeline impact
   Example: "Invoice missing 3 items - SAR 50,000 impact if not addressed"

5. CONVERSATIONAL CHAT
   Input: Any project question in Arabic/English
   Output: Context-aware expert advice
   Example: "What should I do about schedule delays?" → Detailed recommendations

6. SCHEDULE OPTIMIZATION
   Input: Tasks, durations, dependencies, constraints
   Output: Critical path, resource leveling, compression opportunities
   Example: "Compress plumbing by 5 days by increasing crew to 15 workers"

7. COMPLIANCE CHECKING
   Input: Compliance type (Qiwa, Nitaqat, ZATCA, HSE, Balady)
   Output: Compliance status, violations, required actions, deadlines
   Example: "Qiwa: 2 workers exceed 4-month visa limit - renew before June 30"

8. QUALITY RECOMMENDATIONS
   Input: Defect type, location, severity, history
   Output: Root cause, immediate actions, corrections, prevention
   Example: "Concrete cracking - likely curing time issue - add moisture retention"
